What will you learn?

The purpose of this website is to take you through the beautiful and fulfilling journey of fitness! But instead of diving head-first into the nearest gym and performing the most complicated, teeth-grinding lifting session that you can find in old-school lifting magazines, I will offer you the scientific and well-tested approach that will let you orient yourself towards a healthier life, no matter where you find yourself in your fitness journey, here you will find important information about the latest research on the subject. whether you are a first-time gym goer or a more advanced lifter, these concepts and investigations will certainly help you. So start by lifting a pen and a piece of paper and let's learn together about the beautiful art of weight training!

Who am I?

My name is Angel Orozco and I'm a certified personal trainer, I'm also an amateur bodybuilder and an overall gym nerd. throughout my lifting years, I've tried to optimize my training and nutrition to get the best out of it as a 100% natural athlete. I specialize in muscle hypertrofy, that's a subject I've studied for the last few years and what I learned from it helped me design a plan that adapts to my always-changing needs and schedules. My main goal is to point athletes to the best and safest practices that will ensure results, but don't get me wrong, there's no magic trick behind it, it's simple but efficient hard work at the correct dosages!
